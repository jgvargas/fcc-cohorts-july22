The Idea:

Uber eats but for services? Like let's say you need a haircut or a tailor... You open an app, select the service you need, put few other filters which narrows your search and the app shows you the possible ppl providing that service in the area/location you are looking for with prices for service. Sort of google maps, but kinda narrowing the search.
